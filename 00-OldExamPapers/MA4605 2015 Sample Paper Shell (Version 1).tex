\[ \hat{C}_p = \frac{\mbox{USL} - \mbox{LSL}}{6s}\]

\[ \hat{C}_{pk} = \mbox{min} \left[\frac{\mbox{USL} - \bar{x}}{3s},\frac{\bar{x} - \mbox{LSL}}{3s} \right] \]

\[ \hat{C}_{pm} = \frac{\mbox{USL} - \mbox{LSL}}{6\sqrt{s^2+(\bar{x}-T)^2}}\]
